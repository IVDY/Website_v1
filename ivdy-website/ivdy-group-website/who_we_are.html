    Hatice Tavlı

    FOUNDER & INVENTOR

    Hatice began her optimizing healthcare career while studying at the Heinrich Heine Universität Düsseldorf in Germany. Over the past 15 years, Hatice has built many algorithms that analyse data to make prediction about possible outcomes, created computer simulations and supervised many projects in healthcare with the purpose of making a shift from prediction to prevention in healthcare. She invented the concept of the global medical electronic healthcare method and founded IVDY.

    Dave Woodward

    CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

    Dave holds degrees from BYU and has served in senior leadership roles across multiple industries prior to joining ClickFunnels in 2015, including serving as Americo's Regional Director in Houston, TX where he oversaw Americo's expansion into 12 states and the company's sale. Dave was the President & owner of Monopolize Inc. for 17 years where he helped grow insurance, investment, mortgage, and real estate businesses through internet marketing and growth expansion.

    Ryan Montgomery

    CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

    Ryan Montgomery began his career in web application development at Rapidparts Inc., gaining unparalleled early experience in the e-commerce space, while also establishing himself as a respected technological leader. Currently working as a COO for ClickFunnels, Ryan is widely known for his technological and operational expertise. Ryan has led an impressive career as a Software Engineer, CTO and now COO in his 15 years working in various industries.

    Clint Adams

    CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

    Before joining ClickFunnels in 2016, Clint Adams served in a variety of accounting and financial reporting capacities in public accounting and industry. Clint began his professional career in public accounting with Cooper Norman, serving in various public accounting roles for 9 years; Clint also worked as a Senior Accountant for Micron Technology and led the financial reporting and accounting for SuccessEtc.com as CFO.
